My background

I studied Computer & Electrical Engineering at the University of Florida, and later returned to earn my MBA. I began my career as an electrical engineer before changing directions to work as a business analyst and project manager for s small software company in the Talent Management industry.

I spent about 10 years consulting with companies on how to use software to do performance reviews, set and align goals, and plan compensation for their organizations. Once I understood how companies pay their employees, I began negotiating my own salary and eventually helping friends and family negotiate their salaries.

I eventually wrote Fearless Salary Negotiation to share my methodology with as many people as possible. And I've spent the past several years coaching High Earners through salary negotiations to make sure they're paid what they're worth.
